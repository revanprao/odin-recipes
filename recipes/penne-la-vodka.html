Penne La Vodka

Description

This is to make sum bomb penne alla vodka pasta

This pasta is rich and creamy coming from Italy and is rich in taste. The recipe is also easy to make and is delecious. Penne alla vodka has a rich tomato taste withthe tartness of heavy cream cutting throught forming a delight in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • water
  • heavy cream
  • tomato paste
  • salt
  • onions

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in pasta water
  2. Put onoins and oil into a spereate pan on medium heat
  3. Once onions brown, add heavy cream and tomoto sauce
  4. Stir until mixed
  5. Add salt, cheese, and boiled pasta
  6. Stir again
